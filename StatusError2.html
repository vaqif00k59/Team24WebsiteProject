About The Hour of Code event

We are planning to host our event at Khyrdalan Secondary School No. 3 on the 10th of December. There will be a total of 30 students. 7th and 8th graders would be perfect for us since students studying at 9 grades or above will have crucial exams, so we would not want to disturb them. Firstly, we are planning to give brief information about our university and the Hour of Code event. After explaining the details, we will direct students to the computers and start solving the problems of Classic Maze Puzzle on code.org. We will assist them when needed.
